Dental Clinic

The dental clinic provides oral and dental care services, such as cleaning, fillings, and treatment of cavities and gum problems

Pediatrics clinic

Our pediatrics clinic offers comprehensive healthcare services for infants, and children, , ensuring their growth, development, and overall well-being.

Physical Therapy

Physical therapy helps improve mobility, relieve pain, and restore physical function after injuries or surgeries

Maternity Centers

Maternity centers provide comprehensive and safe medical care for both mother and baby before, during, and after childbirth

Birthing Center

  • Obstetrics (pregnancy and childbirth care)
  • Midwifery (natural childbirth support)
  • Maternal-Fetal Medicine (high-risk pregnancy management)

  • Lactation Consultation (breastfeeding support)

Physical Therapy Clinic

  • Orthopedic Physical Therapy (injuries to bones, joints, muscles)

  • Sports Rehabilitation (sports injury recovery and prevention)
  • Pediatric Physical Therapy (children's motor development)
  • Geriatric Physical Therapy (helping seniors maintain mobility)
  • Cardiopulmonary Physical Therapy (heart and lung conditions)
