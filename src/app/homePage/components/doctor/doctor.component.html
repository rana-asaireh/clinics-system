Our Specialty

Dental Clinic

  • Orthodontics (braces and teeth alignment)
  • Prosthodontics (crowns, bridges, dentures)
  • Periodontics (gum disease treatment)
  • Endodontics (root canal therapy)
  • Pediatric Dentistry (dental care for children)
  • Oral Surgery (tooth extraction, minor surgeries)

Pediatrics Clinic

  • Neonatology (care for newborns and premature babies)
  • Pediatric Cardiology (heart care for children)
  • Pediatric Neurology (brain and nervous system care)
  • Pediatric Infectious Disease (treatment of infections)
  • Pediatric Allergy and Immunology (allergies and immune system care)
  • Pediatric Endocrinology (hormonal issues like growth or diabetes)

Birthing Center

  • Obstetrics (pregnancy and childbirth care)
  • Midwifery (natural childbirth support)
  • Maternal-Fetal Medicine (high-risk pregnancy management)
  • Neonatal Care (care for newborns immediately after birth)
  • Lactation Consultation (breastfeeding support)
  • Prenatal Education Specialists (classes and preparation for childbirth)

Physical Therapy Clinic

  • Orthopedic Physical Therapy (injuries to bones, joints, muscles)
  • Neurological Physical Therapy (stroke, spinal cord injury recovery)
  • Sports Rehabilitation (sports injury recovery and prevention)
  • Pediatric Physical Therapy (children's motor development)
  • Geriatric Physical Therapy (helping seniors maintain mobility)
  • Cardiopulmonary Physical Therapy (heart and lung conditions)
